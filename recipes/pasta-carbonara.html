Pasta Carbonara

Description

Authentic Italian pasta carbonara with a rich, creamy sauce made from eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Simple ingredients that create an unforgettable dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 8 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions
  3. While pasta cooks, sauté pancetta until crispy
  4. Whisk eggs and both cheeses together in a bowl
  5. Add plenty of black pepper to the egg mixture
  6. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
  7. Add hot pasta to the pan with pancetta
  8. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture
  9. Add pasta water gradually to create creamy sauce
  10. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper
